Welcome to Pondicherry University Hostel Management System

Our Hostel Management System is designed to provide a seamless experience for both students and administrators. Easily check room availability, manage bookings, and stay updated with all hostel-related information. Whether you are a new student looking for accommodation or an administrator managing multiple hostels, our system offers the tools you need to ensure a comfortable and organized living environment.

Features include:

  • Real-time room availability status
  • Online booking and payment options
  • Comprehensive admin dashboard
  • Warden contact information and support
  • Detailed hostel profiles and amenities

Navigate through the menu to explore different hostels, log in to your account, or contact us for more information. We are committed to making your stay at Pondicherry University comfortable and enjoyable.
